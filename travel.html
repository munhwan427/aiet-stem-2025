Getting to Jeju Island

Your Domestic Flight Has Been Arranged

The organizing committee will book your domestic flight from Gimpo Airport (GMP) to Jeju Airport (CJU). Your flight itinerary will be sent to you separately.

Transfer Assistance Provided

After arriving at Incheon International Airport (ICN), a representative from Jeju National University will meet you and guide you directly to Gimpo Airport (GMP) for your domestic flight to Jeju.

Please keep your mobile phone available so the JNU representative can contact you upon your arrival at Incheon Airport.

WhatsApp Contact: +82 10-5698-6867

Upon Arrival at Jeju Airport

Important: Airport Pickup Service

A representative from Jeju National University will meet you at Jeju International Airport (CJU) and pick you up directly. You do not need to arrange your own transportation from the airport.

Please keep your mobile phone available so the JNU representative can contact you upon your arrival.

Please share your flight details (flight number and arrival time) with the organizing committee in advance so we can arrange your airport pickup.

December Weather in Jeju


5-10°C
41-50°F • Mild Winter

Jeju has a milder winter compared to mainland Korea. Expect cool temperatures with occasional rain. Bring layers and a light jacket.

Travel Tips

  • 1

    Visa Requirements

    Check visa requirements for South Korea based on your nationality. Many countries have visa-free access for short stays.

  • 2

    Currency

    South Korean Won (KRW). Credit cards are widely accepted. ATMs available at airport and hotels.

  • 3

    SIM Card / WiFi

    Purchase a Korean SIM card or rent a portable WiFi device at the airport for internet access.

  • 4

    Language

    Korean is the primary language. English is spoken in hotels and tourist areas. Translation apps are helpful.

About Jeju Island

Jeju Island is South Korea's largest island and a UNESCO World Heritage site, known for its volcanic landscapes, pristine beaches, and unique culture. Often called the "Hawaii of South Korea," it offers a perfect blend of natural beauty and modern infrastructure.
