About the Initiative

Bringing together global leaders to shape the future of AI in education

Purpose

To respond to the growing influence of Artificial Intelligence and Emerging Technologies (AIET) across all areas of Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics Education (STEM), this Global Initiative brings together leading scholars to define the core agenda for AIET in STEM and develop a practical, actionable roadmap for guiding global dialogue around future AIET in STEM.

Theme

This Global initiative brings together educators, researchers, and innovators to examine how education and collaborative efforts can guide the integration of artificial intelligence into K-16 STEM education. Through presentations and interactive discussions, we aim to foster shared understanding, promote ethical practices, and develop innovative solutions for a future where humans lead leveraging AI as an intellectual partner.

Key Deliverables

Outcomes that will shape the future of AI in STEM education globally

Annual Global Forum

Establish the foundation and build a sustainable structure for an annual Global Forum and Conference on AIET in STEM over the next five years, co-hosted with leading international institutes and Jeju National University.

Journal & Publications

Develop strategic plan to launch a new peer-reviewed journal, conference proceedings, and related publications to advance global collaboration and knowledge sharing in AIET and STEM.

Open Forum Sessions

  • AI in the Classroom: Opportunities, Risks, and Building Literacy
  • AI in STEM: Building Skills for Science, Mathematics, and Beyond
  • Panel Discussion: From Vision to Action - A Global Roadmap for AI and Emerging Technologies in STEM Education
